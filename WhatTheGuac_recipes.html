You will need:

4 decent-sized avocados - Hass avocados are a great choice
4 tbsp lime juice - freshly squeezed lime juice is a great choice!
1 tsp salt - we recommend sea salt!
1 tsp lemon pepper
3 tbsp garlic - we recommend freshly minced garlic!
2 tsp 24 Seasoning Salute or any all-seasoning blend of your choice


First, carefully cut each avocado in half and scoop out the inside into a large bowl. Discard the outer skin and the pit, unless you want to try to grow that pit into your own avocado tree - visit our Grow page to learn how!

Second, use a spoon or a fork to gently mash the avocado, but not too much! Leave it a little chunky.

Third, add the rest of the ingredients and mix them all in! You can choose to mash the avocados more during this step to create a smoother blend, but we recommend leaving it chunky!

Lastly, chill in the refridgerator for about 20 minutes and then enjoy with your favorite chips! We recommend a sturdy tortilla chip brand such as Chica Chips to prevent chip breakage! Enjoy!

You will need:

½ ripe avocado
1 large green apple
1 banana
1 cup baby spinach or kale, loosely packed - or other chopped greens!
½ cup water
1 cup frozen pineapple chunks
10 ice cubes
1 tablespoon lemon juice


First, pit the avocado and scoop out one half of the flesh into the blender. Tip: To easily remove the pit, use a knife and firmly knock the blade into the pit until it is slightly wedged inside. Twist the knife while it’s still inside to take the pit out.

Second, chop the apple, leaving the skin on. Break the banana into pieces and place them both in the blender. Add the water and baby kale leaves. Blend until smooth.

Lastly, add the ice, frozen pineapple or mango, and lemon juice. Blend again until smooth, then serve in a cup as is!

We recommend finishing it immediately or store 1 to 2 days in a sealed jar; if it separates, shake to re-integrate it.

You will need:

1 can tuna - water packed or oil packed work
½ ripe avocado
Celery
Red onion
Dill pickles - the key to getting that classic tangy flavor!
Lemon juice - for a little pop
Dijon mustard - it adds just the right savory note


All you have to do is chop the veggies, then mash the ingredients together with a fork. This might just win the easiest meal ever award!

Main dish salad

Crostini or crackers

Avocado bowl
What to do with the other half of your avocado? Use it for serving your tuna salad!
